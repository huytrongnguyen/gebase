# SCENARIO: ALTRIA EPISODE 3

<div class="post-body entry-content float-container" id="post-body-2318167011324307578">
Altria Episode 3 (Cielo y Tierra) scenario was added in v30.84.45. This guide is based on USA client v30.93.37. You do not need any item or raid mission. You will need to kill monsters in the following maps:<br>
<ul>
<li>Camellia Teia</li>
<li>River of Fire</li>
<li>Portus</li>
</ul>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Proposal of the Watcher of the Stars</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed <a href="https://starstorm-ge.blogspot.com/2019/06/altria2scenario.html">Altria Episode 2</a> scenario. <br>
<ol>
<li>Receive quest mail by moving to any town.</li>
<li>Talk to Leona in Frourio (I8).</li>
<li>Talk to Koa in River of Fire (C5).</li>
<li>Talk to the Watcher of the Stars in River of Fire (D2).</li>
<li>Talk to Leona in Frourio (I8) to start a non-combat mission ("A Deal of the Watcher of the Stars").</li>
</ol>
<br>
<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Shackles</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
<ol>
<li>Talk to JD in Frourio (E4).</li>
<li>Kill Phantom of Orden (human AR77 DR75) x5 in Camellia Teia.</li>
<ul>
<li>Click on the tombstones scattered throughout Camellia Teia to summon the phantom.</li>
<li>Phantom of Orden has low DEF (160) and high RES (100), so use physical attackers.</li>
</ul>
<li>Talk to JD in Frourio (E4).</li>
<li>Talk to Hildegard in Frourio (I3).</li>
<li>Collect Incuse of Orden (quest item) x100 by killing Flava (10%), Fleeky (10%), Flua (10%), or Fluham (10%) in River of Fire.</li>
<li>Talk to Hildegard in Frourio (I3) to give the above items.</li>
</ol>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Quiritatio Heimdall</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
<ol>
<li>Start a combat mission ("Defenders of Heimdall", 10 minutes) in Quiritatio Heimdall (J7).</li>
<ul>
<li>Reduce all 4 Quiritatio Defenders (human) to 50% HP.</li>
<li>Mission fails if any one of them dies.</li>
<li>Tip: They have low DEF (102) and high RES (95), so you may want to use melee or shooting characters.</li>
<li>Note: You can enter Quiritatio Heimdall via the portal in River of Fire (D2). There is a limit on how many times you can enter (10 times). Quest warp function is disabled for Quiritatio Heimdall. You will be moved back to River of Fire after the mission.</li>
</ul>
<li value="2">Talk to the Watcher of the Stars in River of Fire (D2).</li>
</ol>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Gathering Quiritatio Information Part 1</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
<ol>
<li>Talk to Marchetti in Frourio (E5).</li>
<li>Start a quest dialogue in Quiritatio Heimdall (E10) to gain Collect Armor Piece of Defender (quest item).</li>
<ul>
<li>Avoid the red/orange splash effects while you are moving. If you come too close to one, a Quiritatio Defender will appear and teleport you back to the entrance (J8). The running Quiritatio Defenders can be ignored.</li>
<li>After you complete this part of the quest, moving into the splash effects will grant you Lv.12 Sprint buff instead.</li>
</ul>
<li>Talk to Marchetti in Frourio (E5) to give the above item.</li>
<li>Talk to Maria in Frourio (E5).</li>
<li>Collect Dried Bloodstain (quest item) x500 by killing Slave Chief (10%), Slave Stabber (10%), Slave Sorcerer (10%), Necro Bow (10%), or Necro Scythe (10%) in Portus.</li>
<li>Talk to Maria in Frourio (E5) to give the above items.</li>
<li>Talk to Marchetti in Frourio (E5).</li>
</ol>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Gathering Quiritatio Information Part 2</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
Reward: Elemental Jewel x3, Moon Stone x3.<br>
<ol>
<li>Talk to Noah in Frourio (I6).</li>
<li>Start a combat mission ("Legacy of Leah", 20 minutes) in Quiritatio Heimdall (F3).</li>
<ul>
<li>Defeat Brontes (lifeless) to gain Necklace of Leah (quest item).</li>
<li>Tip: If Brontes attacks the same target, its ATK increases. Run out of its area skill. It has high RES (95), so you may want to use melee or shooting characters.</li>
<li>Tip: You will be moved back to River of Fire after the mission.</li>
</ul>
<li value="3">Talk to Noah in Frourio (I6) to give the above item.</li>
<li>Talk to Leona in Frourio (I8) for reward.</li>
<ul>
<li>You may now freely enter Quiritatio Heimdall.</li>
</ul>
</ol>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] Quiritatio Exploration</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
<ol>
<li>Talk to the Watcher of the Stars in River of Fire (D2).</li>
<li>Start a combat mission ("Equality", 20 minutes) in Quiritatio Heimdall (F3).</li>
<ul>
<li>Defeat Cassandra (human) and the Quiritatio Guardian Soldiers (human).</li>
<li>Tip: The soldiers have low DEF (99) and high RES (95). You can inflict Blood Curse status on Cassandra.</li>
</ul>
<li>Talk to Cassandra in Quiritatio Heimdall (E6) to start a combat mission ("Test of Cassandra", 20 minutes).</li>
<ul>
<li>Defeat Cassandra (human).</li>
</ul>
</ol>

<b>[130][Cielo y Tierra] The Choice of Cassandra</b><br>
Prerequisites: Completed the quest above.<br>
<ol>
<li>Talk to Cassandra in Quiritatio Heimdall (E6) to make a choice:</li>
<ul>
<li>"A will to survive."</li>
<li>"Relying on each other."</li>
<li>Note: The choice didn't seem to make a difference…</li>
</ul>
<li value="2">Start a combat mission ("Quiritatio Guardians", 80 minutes) in Quiritatio Heimdall (F8).</li>
<ul>
<li>Defeat Labid (undead AR72 DR70), Dorr (wildlife AR72 DR70), Kranus (lifeless AR74 DR72), and Beast Jurgen (wildlife AR73 DR71).</li>
<li>After a short dialogue, defeat Labid (undead), Dorr (wildlife), Kranus (lifeless), Beast Jurgen (wildlife) again. They gain AR+5 and DR+5. Leona and other NPCs will help you.</li>
<li>Tip: The monsters have the same HP and DEF (248). Most RES are 80. Labid has low Mental RES (50) and Ice RES (55). Dorr has low Fire RES (55). Kranus has high RES (All 100). You might want to take out Dorr first since he can inflict Stun status.</li>
<li>Tip: Some NPCs can inflict Armor Crash or Mark status.</li>
<li>Tip: You can inflict Blood Curse status on them.</li>
</ul>
</ol>
